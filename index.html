Plantsasri Indonesia is one of the exporters of agricultural products, especially ornamental plants in Indonesia. Plantsasri Indonesia also cares about all Indonesian farmers. Through this website development program, we provide access to farmers to be able to reach global markets and expand their business reach. Plantsasri Indonesia also has an ornamental plant marketplace that can be used to send and buy ornamental plants and send them wherever they want.
